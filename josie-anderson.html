Josie's testimony

My youth pastor mentored me and showed me how to walk in a relationship with the Lord. From that point I finally started to dive deep into the word and grow in my faith. I have made countless mistakes, but the Lord has taught me so many valuable lessons these past few years. Even just being at liberty for a year, I have grown in so many ways and I cannot wait to see what this upcoming year has in store for me.
